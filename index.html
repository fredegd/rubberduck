Rubberduck Friends

Rubberduck is a popular fan website dedicated to the iconic yellow rubber duck. With a thriving community of fans, the website offers interesting articles, facts, and trivia about rubber ducks. Its online store exclusively sells the original yellow rubber duck, which has become a beloved and collectible item for many. Whether you're a long-time fan or a newcomer to the world of rubber ducks, Rubberduck is a must-visit site for anyone who appreciates the simple pleasures in life.
